Desperate Housewives S01 E01

  1. My name is Mary Alice Young. When you read this morning's paper, you may come across an article about the unusual day I had last week.

    come across: 偶然发现。

  2. Normally, there's never anything newsworthy about my life, ...

    newsworthy: 有报道价值的。

  3. I performed my chores.

    chores: 家务活，琐事。在这里，perform 是完成的意思。

  4. I ran my errands.

    errand: 使命，差事。
    run errand: 跑腿。

  5. Her curiosity aroused, Mrs. Huber tried to think of a reason for dropping in on me unannounced.

    drop in on: 顺道拜访某人。

  6. And, for a moment, Mrs. Huber stood motionless in her kitchen, grief-stricken by this senseless tragedy.

    grief-stricken: grief 为悲痛，忧伤，stricken 为受打击，受灾的，二者加在一起意思为：悲痛欲绝。
    senseless: 愚蠢的，无知觉的，无意识的，不理解的。
    tragedy: 悲剧，灾难。

  7. If there was one thing Mrs. Huber was known for...

    known for: 因……而闻名，以……见长。

  8. I was laid to rest on a Monday.

    lay to rest: 埋葬，安息，入土为安。
